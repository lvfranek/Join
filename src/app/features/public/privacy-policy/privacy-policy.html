Privacy Policy

Data Protection

General Information

The protection of your personal data is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Data Collection

We collect personal data when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Log in to the platform
  • Use the Kanban board features
  • Contact us

Types of Data Collected

The data we may collect includes:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Login credentials
  • Task and board data created by users
  • Technical data (e.g. IP address, browser type)

Purpose of Data Processing

We process your data for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the platform
  • Managing user accounts
  • Improving functionality and user experience
  • Ensuring security and preventing abuse

Legal Basis for Processing

Processing of personal data is based on:

  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR – performance of a contract
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR – legitimate interests

Data Storage

We store your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes stated above or as required by law.

Guest Access

You may use parts of the platform without registration. In this case, only limited technical data may be processed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties unless required by law or necessary for the operation of the service.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and ensure proper functionality. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time.
